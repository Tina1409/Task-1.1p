Project Description-

This project explores coordinated robot interactions using Arduino boards in the intriguing field of swarm robotics. The main goal is to establish a master-slave system in which one or more slave robots are led and controlled by a master robot. Through this project, we examine the possibility of robot cooperation, communication, and collective behaviour.

Project Description-

A thorough project called the Arduino Environmental Impact Monitoring System uses a variety of sensors, including TDS (Total Dissolved Solids), MQ135 (Air Quality), and DHT (Temperature and Humidity) sensors, to detect and analyse several environmental data. Real-time data collecting and analysis are the goals of this initiative, which seeks to increase environmental awareness and encourage responsible behaviour.
